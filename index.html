Questions?

We've got answers!

  • When and Where Does Joe Meet?
    Joe Thirty is held at 8am on the 2nd Wednesday of every month on the 20th Floor in the Enquirer Building.

    Parking is available at The Enquirer's garage on 312 Elm Street. Take the garage elevators down to the third floor. Turn left, walk through the doors, and you will be in the Enquirer's lobby. Turn left, walk past the guard desk, and take another left for the first bank of elevators. Take the elevator up to the 20th floor and you'll be directed to the meeting room.

  • As a member of the community, what do I need to bring?
    Show up!

    Just bring an appetite for breakfast snacks, coffee and the willingness to help. Entrepreneurs love feedback and aren't afraid to ask questions, so don't hold back and let them know how you can help!

  • Does it cost me anything?
    Nope!

    It is completely free. Joe has partnered with the Enquirer, Greater Cincinnati Venture Association and Differential in an effort to help our community come together and help.
